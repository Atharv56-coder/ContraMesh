SaaS SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)

§3. SLA CREDIT POLICY
In the event that service availability drops below 99.9% during any 30-day billing cycle, the Customer shall be entitled to credits.

§4. SLA REPORTING DEADLINE
To receive SLA credits, the Customer must submit a formal credit claim and outage log within 20 days prior to the end of the billing cycle in which the outage occurs.

§9. INDEMNITY AND CAPPED LIABILITY
The Service Provider's total aggregate liability for all claims is limited to $100. The Customer shall defend, indemnify, and hold harmless the Service Provider from any third-party claims without limit.

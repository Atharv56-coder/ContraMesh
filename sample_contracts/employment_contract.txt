§4. VACATION REQUEST SCHEDULE
Employees must request approval for any vacation dates at least 14 days in advance of the planned start date.

§5. VACATION APPROVAL RULES
The Employer must review and approve/deny the vacation request within 3 days of employee submission. This approval is only valid if processed within the physical payroll catalog cycle, which operates strictly on the 1st of every month (30-day cycle).

§10. CONTRACT DISSOLUTION NOTICE
The Employer may terminate this contract immediately at any time with 1 day of severance pay. The Employee must provide a minimum of 90 days written notice to terminate their employment.

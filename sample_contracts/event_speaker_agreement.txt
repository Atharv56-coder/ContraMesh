EVENT SPEAKER AGREEMENT

§2. SLIDE SUBMISSION SCHEDULE
The Speaker must submit all presentation slides to the Event Coordinator at least 10 days prior to the event date.

§3. SLIDE REVIEW CYCLE
The Coordinator shall review slides and issue required change requests within 7 days of slide receipt.

§4. REVISION SUBMISSION
If slides require revisions, the Speaker must resubmit the updated and corrected files at least 12 days prior to the event date to secure their speaking slot.

§8. LIABILITY AND CANCELLATION
The Coordinator may cancel the Speaker's slot at any time without penalty or travel reimbursement. The Speaker cannot cancel their slot unless they pay a 2x slot cancellation fee.

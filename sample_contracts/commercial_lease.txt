COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

§2. TENANT MAINTENANCE OBLIGATIONS
The Tenant must complete all structural and electrical repairs within 10 days of receiving any defect notice from the Landlord. Failure to do so constitutes material breach.

§3. APPROVAL AND PERMISSION CONTROLS
No repairs, modifications, or building operations may be initiated by the Tenant without obtaining the Landlord's written approval. The Landlord has exactly 15 days to review, approve, or reject any repair request.

§4. TERMINATION NOTICE
The Landlord may terminate this lease immediately and without notice if structural issues persist. The Tenant must provide a minimum of 120 days written notice prior to vacating the premises.
